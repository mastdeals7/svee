10. PRODUCT PAGE CONTENT GUIDANCE
==================================================

Use these product notes:

Rice DDGS:
Rice DDGS is a high-protein, high-fiber by-product from rice-based ethanol production. It is used as an ingredient in animal feed. Write for Indonesian feed mills and animal feed buyers. Mention that protein, fiber, moisture, packing and origin must be confirmed before shipment.

Maize Grits / Corn Grits:
Maize grits are coarse ground particles made from cleaned and milled corn. They are used in extruded snacks, breakfast cereals, breweries, confectionery, baking applications and selected feed/industrial use depending on grade. Use both terms naturally: maize grits and corn grits.

Rice Grits:
Rice grits are coarser broken rice particles prepared by grinding and sieving raw rice. They are used in extruded snacks, namkeen, bakery items, instant mixes, ready-to-eat products and food processing applications.

Corn Flour:
Corn flour is used in gluten-free quick breads, baking, pasta making, sausages as starch substitute, papad making and selected cattle feed applications depending on grade.

Rice Flour:
Rice flour is fine powder prepared from raw rice. It is used in snacks, bakery items, instant mixes, ready-to-eat products and traditional food applications.

Flaking Grits:
First-cut de-germed corn grits used for corn flakes, brewery flakes and food processing applications.

Corn Meal:
Fine corn grits below 1 mm used for specialty snacks and efficient extrusion.

Cattle Feed Fibre / Germ / Flour:
Corn fibre, germ and flour can be supplied for cattle feed and feed applications depending on supplier availability and specifications.

Gram Grits / Gram Flour:
Used in extruded snacks and food processing.

Ragi Meal / Ragi Flour:
Used for dosa, roti, idli, upma, puttu, paratha and traditional food products.

Jowar Meal / Jowar Flour:
Used in snacks, chips, extruded products and flour applications.

Multigrain Flour:
Made from a combination of grains such as ragi, barley and corn masa flour. Used in flatbreads and bakery items.

Liquid Glucose:
Used in sweets, confectionery, biscuits, ice cream, jams, jellies, preserves, pastries and related food applications.

Groundnut Kernels:
Indian HPS groundnut kernels are used for oil extraction, direct consumption, roasted snacks, peanut butter, confectionery and food processing.

Sesame Seed:
White and black sesame seeds are used in baking, sweets, confectionery, seasoning and frying applications.
